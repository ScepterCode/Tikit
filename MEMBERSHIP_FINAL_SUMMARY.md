## 📊 System Overview

### Three Tiers
- **Regular (Free)**: Basic features, everyone starts here
- **Special ($10/month)**: Secret Events, priority listing, custom branding, advanced analytics
- **Legend ($30/month)**: Everything + AI bot, marketing automation, API access

### 7-Day Free Trial
- Users can try Special or Legend free for 7 days
- Full feature access during trial
- Payment required after trial ends
- Email reminders on day 6

### Revenue Tracking
- Admin sees total revenue
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
- Tier distribution
- Recent upgrades
- Conversion metrics
